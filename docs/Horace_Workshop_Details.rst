Horace_Workshop_Details
#######################

 The second Horace Workshop will be held at the `ISIS neutron source <http://www.isis.stfc.ac.uk/>`__, Rutherford Appleton Laboratory, U.K. . Accommodation will be provided for all participants at the on-site `Ridgeway House guesthouse <http://www.stfc.ac.uk/about-us/where-we-work/rutherford-appleton-laboratory/ridgeway-house/>`__. Participants must cover their own accommodation and transportation costs. The rooms are pre-booked and are £60 per night, including all meals.
